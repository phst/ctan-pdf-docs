\maketitle

The \textsf{Tabbing} package provides a `\texttt{Tabbing}' environment,
analog to the \LaTeX\ standard `\texttt{tabbing}' environment,
but allowing accented letters. No more \verb|\a'|, \verb|\a`|
and \verb|\a=| needed.
